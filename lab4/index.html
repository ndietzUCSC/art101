Pseudocode for self administered injection

Preparation

  1. Walk to sink; stand comfortably.
  2. Place both hands under faucet opening.
  3. Using right hand thumb and fingers, turn faucet handle clockwise to start water.
  4. Adjust handle until steady, moderate water flow.
  5. Slowly move both hands back and forth under water, fingers downward, to wet all surfaces.
  6. Remove hands; with right hand thumb and forefinger, grasp soap dispenser pump.
  7. Press down pump once firmly; release.
  8. Rub palms together, fingers interlaced; cover all surfaces.
  9. Place right palm over left back of hand; rub in circular motion; switch hands.
  10. Interlace fingers, rub palm to palm.
  11. Rub backs of fingers against opposing palms with fingers interlocked.
  12. Rotate thumbs in clasping motions.
  13. Rub fingertips in circular motions on opposite palms.
  14. Rinse both hands thoroughly under running water.
  15. Using right elbow, turn off faucet by twisting handle clockwise.
  16. Dry hands with disposable towel, patting and rubbing all surfaces.
  17. Use towel to open medication vial packaging; discard towel.
  18. Inspect vial visually; with dominant hand, grasp vial firmly by base.

Sterilizing

  1. Using thumb and index of non-dominant hand, tear open alcohol swab packet.
  2. Remove swab; hold between thumb and index of dominant hand.
  3. Press swab tip onto vial stopper; wipe in slow clockwise circular motion three times.
  4. Remove swab carefully; do not re-touch stopper.

Drawing Medication

  1. Remove syringe from package using dominant hand fingers; avoid touching needle.
  2. Pull needle cap off by grasping it between thumb and index, pulling straight.
  3. Insert needle through vial stopper perpendicular with dominant hand.
  4. Invert vial and syringe together; pull plunger back with thumb and fingers slowly to fill syringe with prescribed volume.
  5. Tap syringe gently with fingers to release air bubbles.
  6. Push plunger slowly to expel air and excess medication to prescribed dose.

Needle Change

  1. Recap needle by carefully placing needle tip into cap without touching outside.
  2. Pull syringe needle and cap off vial without twisting.
  3. With thumb and index finger, remove original needle by holding syringe barrel securely and pulling needle straight.
  4. Take new needle; press onto syringe tip firmly until secure.

Injection

  1. Select injection site; clean with new alcohol swab using circular motion for 15 seconds; allow to air dry.
  2. Hold syringe like a pencil between thumb and fingers.
  3. Stretch skin of injection site taut with non-dominant hand.
  4. Insert needle into skin quickly at prescribed angle.
  5. With thumb, depress plunger steadily to inject medication slowly.
  6. Withdraw needle swiftly at same angle.
  7. Immediately apply gentle pressure with clean gauze or cotton ball.

Cleanup

  1. Engage safety mechanism on needle if available.
  2. Place syringe and needle immediately into sharps container.
  3. Discard all disposables (alcohol swabs, packaging) into biohazard waste.
  4. Wash hands again as in steps 2-15 to conclude."

Memory, Card Matching Game

Still generated by GPT 4.1, but with less detail, to emulate the detail level of code comments.
  1. Shuffle the cards thoroughly to randomize their order.
  2. Lay all cards face down on the table.
  3. Player flips two cards per turn.
  4. If they match, keep the pair.
  5. Check if the flipped cards match.
  6. Player takes another turn after a match.
  7. If they do not match, flip the cards back.
  8. Pass the turn to the next player.
  9. Continue until all pairs are matched.
  10. Count each player’s pairs.
  11. Determine the player with the most pairs as the winner.
  12. Display “Congratulations! You matched all the pairs!”.
